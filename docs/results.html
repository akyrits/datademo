Takeaways

  • Transfer learning works: Using a pretrained AlexNet yields strong performance with relatively few images.
  • Data quality is critical: Misclassifications cluster around blurry, low-light, or ambiguous images.
  • Balanced classes help: Keeping similar numbers of happy and stressed images prevents majority-class bias.
  • Future work: Move beyond binary labels and explore video-based emotion recognition.
